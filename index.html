About Us

About Rajwada

At Rajwada Restaurant, we offer an unforgettable Rajasthani dining experience. Our beautifully designed space reflects the rich heritage of Rajasthan, creating a serene and elegant atmosphere. We pride ourselves on serving authentic Rajasthani cuisine, featuring dishes like Dal Bati Churma, Moth Bajra Ka Kichda , and Jodhpuri Gatta Curry all prepared with traditional Rajasthani hospitality and contemporary comforts.
